## Executive summary (for experts)

We have a **working ML entry timing model** (S1: “will BN move ~100 pts in ~5 min?”) and a **fair historical replay harness** (~540 trades, May–Jul 2024). Portfolio PF tops out around **1.04** (target 1.3). We **cannot** get reliable **CE vs PE** from current direction ML (holdout AUC ~**0.55**). Exit experiments help at the margin; **July drawdown** and **TIME_STOP losers** remain the main drag.

**Best reproducible run:** **E2 dyn_exit** — `32b01989`, 540 trades, PF **1.04**, +6.8% cap, Jul **−15%**.  
**Latest attempt (E5 direction consensus):** **failed** — `2632cdc7`, only **169** trades (**May only**), PF **0.79**, −7.1% cap. Not comparable to E1–E4 (thin book + gates stopped Jun/Jul).

**Ask for experts:**

1. **Direction** — Is CE/PE at entry bar learnable from our features, or should we pivot to **structure/trap** labels (E5-S1) instead of S2 direction ML?
2. **Gates** — `avoid_veto` (IV) blocks ~2k snapshots/run; eval harness vs production caps — what is a fair OOS protocol?
3. **Risk state** — Why did E5 trade **only May** then go flat (0 trades May 29 → Jul)? Risk pause, session cap carry, or replay bug?
4. **Exits** — TIME_STOP = ~60% of trades, PF ~0.15 on that bucket; dyn_exit (E2) helps but not enough for Jul — partial exits, premium-flow exits, or shorter thesis window?
5. **Ship decision** — Is **E2 profile** (`trader_master_ml_entry_v1_dyn_exit`) acceptable for next OOS window, or block on direction/PE leg PF?
