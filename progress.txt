    - open_position nulled stop_price when underlying_stop_pct was set.
    - update() used `elif underlying_... is None and premium_stop_hit`.
  So enabling underlying stops silently DISABLED premium stops.
  Same pattern for targets.
  Fix: always seed premium hard stop_price; allow EITHER underlying OR
       premium check to fire (layered). See tracker.py lines 68-70, 174-181.
  Tests added: test_premium_stop_still_fires_when_underlying_stop_set_but_not_hit,
               test_premium_target_still_fires_when_underlying_target_set_but_not_hit.
